and stars leaving the birthline.

Authors: R. Behrend and A. Maeder
         Geneva Observatory

Contact: raoul.behrend@obs.unige.ch
         andre.maeder@obs.unige.ch
 or see: http://obswww.unige.ch

Entries of the tables:
  -  time in year
  -  decimal logarithm of the bolometric luminosity of the star in
     units of solar luminosity
  -  mass of the star in units of solar mass
  -  effective temperature in Kelvin
  -  radius of the star in units of solar radius
  -  upper radius of the convective zone, in units of stellar radius
  -  lower radius of the convective zone, in units of stellar radius
  -  disc luminosity, in units of stellar luminosity

The birtline is computed with \tilde{f}=0.5.
t_stop is the age of the star at the end of the accretion
